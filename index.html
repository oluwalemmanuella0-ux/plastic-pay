Plastic Pay

Recycle Plastic. Earn Points. Redeem Rewards.

About the Project

Plastic Pay is an initiative designed to reduce plastic waste by encouraging people to pick up and deposit plastic in a Reverse Vending Machine (RVM). Each deposit earns points on the Plastic Pay mobile app, and these points can be redeemed for amazing gifts and rewards.

How It Works

1. Collect Plastic

Pick up used plastic bottles, cans, or containers.

2. Deposit in RVM

Drop them into our Reverse Vending Machine near you.

3. Earn Points

Each deposit gives you points on the Plastic Pay app.

4. Redeem Gifts

Exchange your points for exciting gifts and rewards.

Our Goal

To support the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), especially *Responsible Consumption & Production (SDG 12)* and *Climate Action (SDG 13)* by reducing plastic pollution and promoting recycling.
